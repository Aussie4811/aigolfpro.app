Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Introduction

AI Golf Pro ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and website (collectively, the "Service"). By using AI Golf Pro, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Who We Are

AI Golf Pro is a golf coaching application powered by Ace, your personal AI Golf Professional. We operate under Canadian privacy law (PIPEDA). We are based in Winnipeg, Manitoba, Canada. For privacy inquiries, contact us at: aigolfpro@proton.me

3. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, including:

  • Account information (name, email address, password)
  • Golf profile data (handicap, experience level, goals, dominant hand)
  • Golf performance data (launch monitor sessions, round scores, swing videos, shot dispersion data)
  • Fitness and health data (movement screening results, fitness plans, workout sessions)
  • Communications with our AI coach, Ace
  • Social content you post to The Clubhouse (posts, comments, likes)
  • Device information and usage analytics
  • Waitlist email addresses collected prior to launch

4. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Service
  • Personalise your AI coaching experience with Ace
  • Process your golf data to generate coaching recommendations
  • Display your posts and activity in The Clubhouse social feed
  • Send you service-related communications
  • Monitor usage patterns to improve the app
  • Comply with legal obligations

5. AI Processing

Our AI coaching features are powered by Anthropic's Claude AI. When you interact with Ace or submit data for analysis, that data is processed by Anthropic's systems in accordance with their privacy policy. We do not use your personal golf data to train AI models.

6. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase (PostgreSQL database hosted on AWS) and Cloudflare R2 (video and image storage). We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest (AES-256).

7. Video and Image Storage

Swing videos you upload are stored securely in Cloudflare R2. Videos are private by default and only accessible to you. Images posted to The Clubhouse are stored in Supabase Storage and are publicly viewable as part of the social feed. You can delete your content at any time from within the app.

8. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information with:

  • Supabase — database and storage infrastructure
  • Cloudflare — content delivery and video storage
  • Anthropic — AI processing for Ace coaching features
  • Law enforcement when required by applicable law

9. Social Features

Content you post to The Clubhouse (posts, comments, images) is visible to other authenticated users of the app. Your first name and avatar are displayed alongside your posts. You can delete your posts and comments at any time. You can block other users to prevent their content from appearing in your feed.

10. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate personal information
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Export your data
  • Object to processing of your personal information

To exercise these rights, use the account deletion feature in the app (Settings → Delete Account) or contact us at privacy@aigolfpro.app. We will action deletion requests within 30 days.

11. Account Deletion

You can delete your account at any time from within the app via Settings → Delete Account. This will permanently delete all your personal data, golf statistics, swing videos, posts, and comments. Swing videos and photos are deleted from our storage immediately. Remaining data is purged within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal purposes.

12. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal purposes. Swing videos and photos are deleted from storage immediately upon account deletion.

13. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately at privacy@aigolfpro.app.

14. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by posting the new policy and updating the date above. Your continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

15. Contact Us

Questions or privacy requests? Contact us at aigolfpro@proton.me
