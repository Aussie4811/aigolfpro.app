Last updated: March 2026

AI Golf Pro ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and website (collectively, the "Service").

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, including:

  • Account information (name, email address, password)
  • Golf profile data (handicap, experience level, goals)
  • Golf performance data (launch monitor sessions, round scores, swing videos)
  • Fitness and health data (movement screening results, fitness plans)
  • Communications with our AI coach, Ace
  • Waitlist email addresses collected prior to launch

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Service
  • Personalise your AI coaching experience
  • Process your golf data to generate coaching recommendations
  • Send you service-related and launch communications
  • Monitor and analyse usage patterns to improve the app

4. AI Processing

Our AI coaching features are powered by Anthropic's Claude AI. When you interact with Ace or submit data for analysis, that data is processed by Anthropic's systems. We do not use your personal golf data to train AI models.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase (PostgreSQL database) and Cloudflare R2 (video storage). We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption in transit and at rest.

6. Video Storage

Swing videos you upload are stored securely in Cloudflare R2. Videos are private by default and only accessible to you. You can delete your videos at any time from within the app.

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information with service providers (Supabase, Cloudflare, Anthropic) necessary to operate the Service, or when required by law.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information at any time. Contact us at [email protected] for any privacy requests.

9. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
